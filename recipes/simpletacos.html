Simple Tacos

Description

The original delicious simple tacos! So easy to make and the simplicity will make none of your friends dislike them.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Tortillas
  • Shredded cheese
  • Any type of sauce you like
  • Any greens you like(optional)

Steps

  1. Place minced beef into a pan over high heat
  2. Once the beef is done remove it and set it aside
  3. Cook up tortillas in the same pan over medium head flipping it every 5-10 seconds until it is done
  4. Now assemble your tacos by placing some beef onto the tortilla and drizzle it with cheese
  5. Optional: Garnish your taco with whatever sauce/greens you want
